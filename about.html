About Me

I’m an event production & management professional who loves turning ideas into smooth, audience-friendly experiences. I plan, coordinate, and run events end-to-end—keeping budgets on track, teams aligned, and timelines tight.

On the ground, I focus on clear checklists, vendor coordination, and zero-drama backstage execution. On the strategy side, I build simple systems for tracking progress, controlling costs, and measuring impact—so every event gets better than the last.

Outside work, I’m into F1, cricket, and football, and I enjoy music festivals. That mix keeps my head in both precision and crowd energy—the two things great shows are made of.
